What's your pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?

My pick of the week is an article detailing the very recent data leak of gaming company Razer, who exposed over 100,000 users personal data over the course of the past few months due to a misconfigured server. What I found most interesting about this article is that despite being notified of their error almost a month ago, the vulnerability in Razer's system was left exposed up until a few days ago. I think this kind of relates to continuous integration, because if Razer had a previous secure configuration build of their server to roll back to and had continuously been testing their network when they made changes, the discrepancy would have been caught much sooner. It also speaks to the moral disregard of technology companies with consumer data, since Razer had plenty of opportunities to fix their security flaw but rather waited until 100,000 people's data was compromised to take action.
