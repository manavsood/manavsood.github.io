About ISM

What is ISM?

The Independent Study and Mentorship Program

The Independent Study and Mentorship program allows high school students to gain valuable career-oriented experience through a professional mentor in their chosen field.

How it works

Through ISM, students begin the process of finding a mentor at the beginning of the year. Throughout the year they conduct research, write reports, and gain experience under their mentor culminating in a Final Project.

Why?

ISM is a truly unique opportunity granted to student leaders passionate about their field.
