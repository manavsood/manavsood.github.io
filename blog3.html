Blog 9/11 to 9/17

My fourth week in the ISM program provided me the most in depth look into Cybersecurity that I’ve had so far. I conducted my first interview with a professional in my field, Mr. William Gordon, a former CIO to NetApp and current CEO of his own Cybersecurity firm, as well as being on the Board of Directors for the Cyber Future Foundation. Despite my anxiousness for conducting my first interview with such a successful professional and leader in my field of study, I had a very informative and enjoyable time speaking with Mr. Gordon about his profession and the Cybersecurity field. He gave me invaluable advice about succeeding in starting a business as well as directing my learning towards more focused areas, such as the rapidly emerging technology of Blockchain, which I conducted my Research Assessment for this week over. Researching blockchain, although a deceptively simple concept to comprehend, raised many questions that I am eager to answer in my next research assessments. I found that meeting with a professional was much more effective in gaining a real-world assessment of the Cybersecurity field as opposed to research, and it will continue to be important in the weeks ahead that I find remarkably intelligent and successful professionals like Mr. Gordon to continue my ISM journey.
